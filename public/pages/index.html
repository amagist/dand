Questions?

What work qualifies?

We use the following criteria used to assess projects for suitability for the service:

- Sponsored by a client exec
- A field technical professional to act as 'product owner'
- Proposal must be focused on must-win clients and enterprise
- Client must be willing to engage in a half day IBM Design Thinking Workshop to kick the project off, and a half day showcase session at the end of the project
- Projects must be able to be completed in under a month.
- It should use technology for which we have expertise available in our team

How much does it cost?

It is free for projects that qualify, depending on availability in the team.
